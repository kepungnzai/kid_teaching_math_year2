The Power of Numbers!

Learn how numbers can grow super fast when we use exponents!

What is a Power? 🤔

A power (or exponent) tells us how many times to multiply a number by itself. It's like a shortcut for writing repeated multiplication!

23 means: 2 × 2 × 2 = 8
The big number (2) is the base. The small number (3) is the exponent.

The Language of Powers 🗣️

We say "2 to the power of 3" or "2 cubed". "2 squared" means 2² = 2 × 2 = 4.

🧊 Cool Fact! In the ancient world, a mathematician named Euclid first wrote about powers over 2,300 years ago! People use them for everything from measuring earthquakes to counting stars in the sky.

Powers of 2: Double Trouble! 🎲

Watch what happens when we keep multiplying by 2:

2³= 8

See the pattern? Each time we add 1 to the exponent, the answer doubles!
It grows fast: 2, 4, 8, 16, 32, 64, 128, 256... 🌱➡️🌳

💡 Key Idea: The exponent tells you how many zeros to put after 1!
10³ = 1 followed by 3 zeros = 1,000  |  10&sup6; = 1 followed by 6 zeros = 1,000,000

🌍 The distance from Earth to the Sun is about 108 kilometers! (That's 100,000,000 km!)

🧬 Your body has about 1014 cells — that's a 1 with 14 zeros! 100 trillion cells!

Super Special Powers! ⚡

Any Number to the Power of 0 = 1

It might seem strange, but any number to the power of 0 is 1!
50 = 1    1000 = 1    1,000,0000 = 1

Any Number to the Power of 1 = Itself

When the exponent is 1, you're not multiplying anything extra.
71 = 7    201 = 20    5001 = 500

Negative Exponents = Fractions!

A negative exponent means 1 divided by the power:
2-1 = ½    10-2 = 1/100 = 0.01

🧠 Power Quiz — Test Yourself!

1. What is 24?
24 = 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 = 16 🎉
2. What is 103?
103 = 10 × 10 × 10 = 1,000 (one thousand) 🎉
3. What is 50? (Tricky!)
1! Any number to the power of 0 equals 1. So 50 = 1. 🧙‍♂️
4. 32 is also called "3 _____"?
3 squared! 32 = 3 × 3 = 9. 📏
5. Which is bigger: 25 or 52?
25 = 32. 52 = 25. So 25 is bigger! 🏆
6. What is 71?
7! Any number to the power of 1 is just itself. ✅
7. What is 106?
106 = 1,000,000 = one million! That's a 1 with 6 zeros. 💰
8. What is 43?
43 = 4 × 4 × 4 = 64 🌟
