What is a Square Root? 🤔

A square root is the opposite of squaring a number. If you square a number (multiply it by itself), the square root brings you back to where you started!

√25 = 5   because   5 × 5 = 25
The √ symbol is called the radical sign. It asks: "What number times itself equals this?"

📐 The square root symbol (√) has been used since the 1500s! It comes from the Latin word "radix" meaning "root" — like the root of a plant that gives it life!

Perfect Squares 🌟

Numbers like 1, 4, 9, 16, 25 are called perfect squares because their square roots are whole numbers!

1√1 = 1
4√4 = 2
9√9 = 3
16√16 = 4
25√25 = 5
36√36 = 6
49√49 = 7
64√64 = 8
81√81 = 9
100√100 = 10

💡 Pattern: Perfect squares are 1, 4, 9, 16, 25, 36... The difference between each one grows by 2 each time!
1 → +3 → 4 → +5 → 9 → +7 → 16 → +9 → 25 → +11 → 36 ...

What About Numbers Like √20? 🤷

Not every number is a perfect square. For example, √20 is between √16 = 4 and √25 = 5.
It's approximately 4.47. These are called irrational numbers — they go on forever without repeating!

Handy Square Root Rules 📏

🔑 Key Rules

1√(a × b) = √a × √b — You can split the square root of a product!
Example: √(4 × 9) = √4 × √9 = 2 × 3 = 6
2√(a ÷ b) = √a ÷ √b — You can split the square root of a division!
Example: √(100 ÷ 4) = √100 ÷ √4 = 10 ÷ 2 = 5
3√a × √a = a — A square root times itself gives the original number!
Example: √7 × √7 = 7
4Every positive number has TWO square roots — a positive and a negative one!
Example: √25 = 5 or -5, because (-5) × (-5) = 25 too!

🧠 Square Root Quiz — Test Yourself!

1. What is √49?
√49 = 7 because 7 × 7 = 49! 🎉
2. What is √81?
√81 = 9 because 9 × 9 = 81! 🎉
3. What is √144?
√144 = 12 because 12 × 12 = 144! 🎉
4. What is √1?
√1 = 1 because 1 × 1 = 1! ✅
5. What is √(9 × 4)? (Use the rule!)
√(9 × 4) = √9 × √4 = 3 × 2 = 6 🧠
6. Which is bigger: √64 or √100?
√100 = 10 is bigger than √64 = 8. The bigger the number, the bigger the square root! 📈
7. What is √0?
√0 = 0 because 0 × 0 = 0! ✅
8. Is 50 a perfect square?
No! 7 × 7 = 49 and 8 × 8 = 64, so 50 is between perfect squares. √50 ≈ 7.07 ❌
