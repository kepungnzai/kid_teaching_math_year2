🍕 Pizza Fractions! 🧮

What are Fractions?

A fraction is a way to talk about a part of a whole. Imagine a delicious pizza 🍕 — if you cut it into pieces, each piece is a fraction of the whole pizza!

The bottom number (denominator) tells you how many equal parts the pizza is cut into. The top number (numerator) tells you how many parts you have.

🍕 What is a Half?

½

A half is when you cut 1 whole pizza into 2 equal pieces!

You take 1 piece out of 2 pieces total. That's why we write it as 1/2 (one-half)!

🧠 Two halves make a whole pizza!

🍕 Whole Pizza

1 whole
A whole pizza — not cut yet!

🍕 One Half

½ (one half)
1 piece out of 2 equal pieces ✨

🧠 Understanding Halves

1 Start with 1 whole pizza. It's all yours! 🍕
2 Cut it into 2 equal pieces. Each piece is the same size! ✂️
3 Take 1 piece. You have 1 out of 2 pieces = ½ (one half)! 🎉

🍕 What is a Quarter?

¼

A quarter is when you cut 1 whole pizza into 4 equal pieces!

You take 1 piece out of 4 pieces total. That's 1/4 (one-quarter)!

🧠 Four quarters make a whole pizza!

🍕 One Quarter

¼ (one quarter)
1 piece out of 4 equal pieces ✨

🍕 Four Quarters

4 × ¼ = 1 whole
Four quarters make a whole pizza! 🎉

🧠 Understanding Quarters

1 Start with 1 whole pizza. Let's share it! 🍕
2 Cut it into 4 equal pieces. Each piece is a quarter! ✂️
3 Take 1 piece. You have 1 out of 4 pieces = ¼ (one quarter)! 🎉

🍕 What Comes After a Quarter?

⅛

An eighth (1/8) comes after a quarter! If you cut a pizza into 8 equal pieces, each piece is one-eighth!

An eighth is half of a quarter. If you cut each quarter in half, you get two eighths! 🤯

🧠 Eight eighths make a whole pizza!

1
🍕 1 Whole Pizza
½
🍕 Half = 2 pieces
¼
🍕 Quarter = 4 pieces
⅛
🍕 Eighth = 8 pieces

🧠 How Halves, Quarters, and Eighths are Related

1 1 whole pizza = 2 halves = 4 quarters = 8 eighths 🤯
2 ½ (half) = 2 quarters = 4 eighths
3 ¼ (quarter) = 2 eighths — cut a quarter in half!

🍕 Real Pizza Story!

📖Pizza Night with Friends!

You ordered 1 large pizza for you and your friend. There are 2 of you.

If you share equally, each person gets half (½) of the pizza! 🍕

But wait! Your friend's mom and dad come home too! Now there are 4 people.

Each person gets one quarter (¼) of the pizza instead! That's a smaller piece. 😊

And if your friend's grandparents also come? That's 6 people! Now each person gets an even smaller piece called one-sixth (⅙).

Moral of the story: The more people you share with, the smaller each fraction becomes! But sharing is always great! 💖
