🧮 Multiply & Divide — Together! 🚀

Multiplication and Division are best friends!

Multiplication is adding the same number over and over (a shortcut!).

Division is sharing equally into groups.

When we use them together, we can solve real-world problems! Let's see how! 💪

🧠 Multiply × Divide = Real-World Problem Solving! 🎯

📚 Quick Refresher

✖️

Multiplication

Repeated addition! If you have 3 bags with 4 apples each:

3 × 4 = 12 🍎
➗

Division

Sharing equally! If 12 apples go into 3 bags:

12 ÷ 3 = 4 🍎

🌍 Real-World Problem

🍪 The Cookie Fundraiser!

You and your friends are selling cookies to raise money for a school trip! 🏫

You have 5 boxes of cookies. Each box has 12 cookies. You want to share them equally among 4 friends to sell.

❓ How many cookies does each friend get to sell?

🧠 Let's Solve It Step by Step!

✖️ Multiply
First, find the total cookies: 5 boxes × 12 cookies = 60 cookies
➗ Divide
Now, share the 60 cookies equally among 4 friends: 60 ÷ 4 = 15 cookies each
✅ Answer
Each friend gets 15 cookies to sell! 🎉

🍪 15 cookies per friend! 🎉
Step 1 (Multiply): 5 boxes × 12 cookies = 60 total cookies
Step 2 (Divide): 60 cookies ÷ 4 friends = 15 each
Check: 15 × 4 = 60 ✅

🧠 The Secret: Multiply Then Divide!

✖️ Multiply to find the total (combine groups)
➗ Divide to share the total equally
💡 Together they solve problems like: "5 boxes × 12 cookies ÷ 4 friends = 15 each!"
