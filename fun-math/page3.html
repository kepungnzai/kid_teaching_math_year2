🇦🇺 Australia's Population Growth! 📈

How much has Australia grown?

Let's travel through time! In 1901, Australia had 3.8 million people. By 2023, that number grew to 26.8 million! That's a huge difference! 🤯

But how much bigger is it? We can use percentage increase to find out! Let's calculate it step by step! 🧮

📊 Australia's population went from 3.8 million to 26.8 million.
What's the percentage increase?

Australia's population increased by approximately 605%! 🎉

🎯 The Answer:

605% increase! (Or approximately 600%)

3.8 M → 26.8 M = ~605% growth over 122 years!

💡 Fun Fact: Australia's population grew more than 7 times its original size! From 3.8 million to 26.8 million people! 🇦🇺✨

🧠 Percentage Increase Formula

1 Difference = New Value − Old Value
2 Divide the difference by the original value
3 Multiply by 100 to get the percentage!
💡 % Increase = (New − Old) ÷ Old × 100
