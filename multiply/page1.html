✖️ Let's Multiply by 2! 🧮

What is Multiplication?

Multiplication is like adding the same number over and over — it's a shortcut for repeated addition! 🚀

If you have 2 groups of apples and each group has 3 apples, instead of saying 3 + 3, you can say 2 × 3 = 6! ✨

Today we'll learn the 2 times table — the easiest and funniest one! 🎉

🍎 2 × 1 = 2

2 × 1 = 2

1 group of 2 apples = 2 apples total! 🍎

2 × 1 = 2
🍎 🍎
1 group of 2

🧠 Understanding 2 × 1

1 2 × 1 means 1 group of 2.
2 It's the same as adding 2 just once: just 2!
3 2 × 1 = 2 — any number × 1 is just itself! 🎉

🍎 2 × 2 = 4

2 × 2 = 4

2 groups of 2 apples = 4 apples total! 🍎🍎

2 × 2 = 4
2 + 2 = 4
🍎 🍎
Group 1
🍎 🍎
Group 2

🧠 Understanding 2 × 2

1 2 × 2 means 2 groups of 2.
2 It's the same as 2 + 2 (adding 2 twice)!
3 2 + 2 = 4, so 2 × 2 = 4! 🎉
