🚗 Rate of Change: How Far Can We Go? 🛣️

What is Rate of Change?

Rate of change tells us how fast something changes over time. 🚀

If a car drives at 50 km every hour, then in 2 hours it travels 100 km! That's multiplication in action! ✨

Let's explore how speed × time = distance! 🎉

🛣️ The Car Trip

🚗 A car drives at a constant speed of 50 km every hour.

This means: in 1 hour → 50 km   |   in 2 hours → 100 km

We can write this as: Speed × Time = Distance

🧮 The Calculation

50 × 1 = 50

In 1 hour at 50 km/h, the car travels 50 km!

🧠 Understanding Rate of Change

1 Rate (speed) tells us how much distance is covered in 1 unit of time (1 hour).
2 To find the total distance, multiply the speed by the time: Speed × Time = Distance.
3 The faster you go or the longer you travel, the farther you get! 🚀
