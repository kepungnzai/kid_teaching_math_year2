🎯 Percentages — Out of 100! ✨

Percentages are just fractions — but with a special base of 100!

The word "percent" comes from Latin: "per centum" meaning "out of 100".

🎯 Half = 50%

½ = 0.5 = 50% — They All Mean the Same Thing!

0% 50 out of 100 = 50% 100%
Fraction½
Decimal0.5
Percent50%

50% means 50 out of 100 — exactly half! If 100 people love pizza, 50% (50 people) said yes! 🍕

🧠 How ½ becomes 50%

1 A percent always means "out of 100". So we ask: ½ = ? out of 100.
2 ½ × 100 = 50 — so ½ is 50 out of 100 = 50%! 🎉
3 Same as decimal: 0.5 × 100 = 50%. Just multiply by 100 and add a % sign!

🎯 Quarter = 25%

¼ = 0.25 = 25% — One Quarter Out of 100!

0% 25 out of 100 = 25% 100%

Decimal0.25

If a pizza has 100 pepperonis, 25% means you get 25 pepperonis — that's a quarter! 🍕

🎯 One Fifth = 20%

⅕ = 0.2 = 20% — One Fifth Out of 100!

20% = 20 out of 100 = ⅕. If you score 20 out of 100 on a test, that's 20%!

💡 The "Out of 100" Pattern:

½ = 50%  |  ¼ = 25%  |  ⅕ = 20%  |  1 = 100%

Percentages make it easy to compare — everything is out of 100! 🎯

🧠 How to Convert Any Fraction to a Percent

1 Take the fraction: ⅕
2 Divide: 1 ÷ 5 = 0.2 (to get the decimal form)
3 Multiply by 100: 0.2 × 100 = 20
4 Add the % sign: 20% 🎉
💡 Quick trick: Multiply the fraction by 100: ⅕ × 100 = 20%! Just ÷ bottom × top!
