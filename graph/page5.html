🚀 Rocket Thrust & Dramatic Drop! 🔥

What is Thrust?

Thrust is the force that pushes something forward or upward — like a rocket engine blasting off into space! The more thrust, the faster it goes up! 🚀

But what goes up must come down! Some things drop dramatically — like a meteor falling from the sky or a skydiver pulling a parachute! 💥

Let's see three objects on a graph: one that shoots up super fast, one that drops super fast, and one that drifts slowly!

🧠 Steep line up = powerful thrust! Steep line down = dramatic drop! 💪

🚀 Meet the Objects!

🚀
Rocket Ship

🔥 Thrust: EXTREME!

Falling Meteor

💥 Drop: EXTREME!
🎈
Floating Balloon

A balloon drifts gently upward and downward. Very little thrust — it just floats along with the wind!
