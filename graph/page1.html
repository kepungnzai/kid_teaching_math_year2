📊 Let's Read Graphs! 🧮

What is a Graph?

A graph is a picture that shows information (data) in a way that's easy to understand! Instead of reading a long list of numbers, you can just look at the bars to compare things quickly! 📈

Graphs are used everywhere — in schools, newspapers, TV, and even video games! They help us answer questions like: "Which animal is most popular?" or "How many cupcakes were sold?"

🧠 Graphs turn numbers into pictures — so we can see the data! 👀

🔍 Parts of a Bar Graph

📏
Y-Axis (Height)

The vertical line on the left. It shows the numbers — like how many or how much!

↔️
X-Axis (Bottom)

The horizontal line at the bottom. It shows the categories — like animal names or days of the week!

📊
Bars

Each bar shows the value for one category. The taller the bar, the bigger the number!

🎯
Title

The title tells you what the graph is about — always read it first!

🧠 How to Read a Bar Graph

1 Read the title — what is this graph about?
2 Look at the bottom (x-axis) — what categories are shown?
3 Look at the side (y-axis) — what numbers are we counting?
4 Compare the bar heights — the tallest bar = the biggest number!
