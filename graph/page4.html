🦅 Bird Speed Showdown! 🐦

How fast can birds fly?

Different birds fly at very different speeds! Some zoom up and down like rockets 🚀, while others glide gently. Let's compare three amazing birds on a line graph and see how their speed changes over time!

Watch how the steepness of each line tells us who's fast and who's slow!

🧠 Steep line = fast change! Flat line = steady speed! Follow the lines to see each bird's story! 👀

🧠 What the Lines Tell Us

1 🦅 Peregrine Falcon — The line goes up very steep (fast rise!) and down very steep (fast drop!). Steep = fast change!
2 🐦 Pigeon — The line goes up and down with gentle slopes. Less steep = slower change!
3 🦃 Ostrich — The line is a straight diagonal going up at the same angle. Constant speed = no change in rate!
4 📊 Compare the steepness! The steeper the line, the faster the change. Flat line = no change!
