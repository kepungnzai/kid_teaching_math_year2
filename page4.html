📖The Pizza Leftover Challenge

Your family orders pizza for movie night! 🎬 There are 13 people at your house, but each large pizza only has 8 slices. You buy 2 pizzas (that's 16 slices total).

The Question: If everyone gets exactly 1 slice, how many slices are left over?

🧮 The Division: You have 16 slices and 13 people. Each person gets 1 slice, and there's a remainder!

This is called division with a remainder — super useful in real life! 🎉

16 ÷ 13 = 1

Each person gets 1 slice! 🍕

+ 3 left over (remainder of 3) 🎉

🧠 Let's Think Step by Step!

1 16 slices total, 13 people.
2 13 fits into 16 exactly 1 time. 13 × 1 = 13.
3 Subtract: 16 − 13 = 3 leftover slices! ✅

🎯 The Answer:

Everyone gets 1 slice + 3 leftover! 🍕

The remainder is 3. You have leftover pizza for breakfast tomorrow! 🥳

💡 Fun Fact: 13 × 1 + 3 = 16. The remainder is what's left when numbers don't divide evenly!

🧠 Remainder Challenge Quiz!

1. 🎂 22 cupcakes for 5 friends. Each gets the same number. How many left over?

✨ 22 ÷ 5 = 4 each, 2 left over! 5 × 4 = 20, 22 − 20 = 2 🧁

2. 🎒 30 pencils for 7 students. Equal share. How many remain?

✨ 30 ÷ 7 = 4 each, 2 remain! 7 × 4 = 28, 30 − 28 = 2 ✏️

3. 🐶 19 dog treats for 6 dogs. Same per dog. How many remainder?

✨ 19 ÷ 6 = 3 each, 1 remainder! 6 × 3 = 18, 19 − 18 = 1 🦴

4. 🎈 37 party balloons for 8 kids. Equal share. What's the remainder?

✨ 37 ÷ 8 = 4 each, 5 remainder! 8 × 4 = 32, 37 − 32 = 5 🎈🎈🎈🎈🎈 Great job!
