💰 Birthday Money Splits! 🎂

📖The Birthday Money Splits

You and your 3 best friends find a magical $20 bill on the playground! 🎉 You all agree to split it equally (fairly).

The Question: How many dollars does each person get?

🧮 The Division: You have $20, and there are 4 people total (you + 3 friends).

20 ÷ 4 = 5

Each person gets $5! 🎉

🧠 Let's Think Step by Step!

1 We have $20 total and 4 people.
2 Think: What number times 4 equals 20?
3 4 × 5 = 20 ✅ So 20 ÷ 4 = 5!

🎯 The Answer:

Everyone gets $5! 🍦

And you all go buy ice cream together! 🎉

💡 Fun Fact: $5 × 4 people = $20 total. Multiplication checks division!
